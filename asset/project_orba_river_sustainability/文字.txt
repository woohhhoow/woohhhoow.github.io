# Project Introduction / 项目介绍

## English

Orba River Sustainability is a regional landscape strategy for restoring the ecological baseline of the Orba River basin. Responding to fragmented habitats, flood vulnerability, agricultural pressure and disturbed extraction sites, the project establishes a continuous blue-green framework along the river corridor. Ecological restoration is combined with recreational spaces and slow-mobility connections, linking the river landscape with nearby communities. Three site-specific interventions translate the territorial strategy into local actions: the regeneration of a former mining area, a new entrance park and an inner-town green corridor.

## 中文

Orba River Sustainability 是一项面向奥尔巴河流域的区域景观规划。项目回应栖息地破碎化、洪水风险、农业生产压力以及采砂遗留场地等问题，通过连续的蓝绿基础设施重建河岸地区的生态基底。设计将洪泛区修复、植被恢复、公共休闲空间与慢行网络结合起来，使河流景观与周边社区重新建立联系。区域尺度的策略进一步落实为三个具有代表性的节点：废弃采砂场改造、城镇入口公园以及贯穿居住区的内部绿色廊道。

## Image 03 — Casal Cermelli Mine Park / 卡萨尔切尔梅利矿坑公园

**EN**

A former extraction site is transformed into a riverside ecological park. Reforestation, floodplain restoration and lightweight recreational paths preserve the traces of the disturbed landscape while introducing new public uses.

**CN**

原有采砂场被改造为河岸生态公园。通过植被恢复、洪泛区修复与轻量化游憩路径，场地在保留扰动地貌痕迹的同时，获得新的公共活动功能。

---

## Image 04 — Town Entrance Park / 城镇入口公园

**EN**

The entrance park creates a transitional landscape between farmland, water channels and the residential edge. Wetland planting, open spaces and cycling routes establish a recognisable gateway while reconnecting the town with its agricultural surroundings.

**CN**

入口公园在农田、水渠与居住区边缘之间构建过渡性景观。湿地种植、开放空间与骑行路径共同形成具有识别度的城镇门户，并重新连接聚落与周边农业环境。

---

## Image 05 — Town Inner Park / 城镇内部公园

**EN**

The inner-town intervention follows the existing wall and canal system to create a linear public landscape. Tree-lined paths, productive gardens and small gathering spaces extend the regional ecological network into the everyday life of the community.

**CN**

城镇内部公园依托原有墙体与水渠系统形成线性公共空间。林荫步道、生产性花园与小型活动场地将区域生态网络延伸至社区日常生活之中。
