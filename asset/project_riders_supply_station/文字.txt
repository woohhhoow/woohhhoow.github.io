Project Introduction / 项目介绍
English

RIDERS' SUPPLY STATION is an urban service design project created for takeaway delivery riders and local citizens in Wenjiang District, Chengdu. The project responds to the lack of resting space, parking areas, charging facilities, and daily support infrastructure for riders in high-density urban environments. By transforming overlooked urban gaps into a modular network of supply stations, the design provides spaces for rest, recharge, parking, communication, and community interaction. Through the combination of physical infrastructure and a smart digital platform, the project proposes a more inclusive and rider-friendly urban support system.

中文

骑手补给站 是一个面向成都温江区外卖骑手与城市居民的公共服务设计项目。项目回应了高密度城市环境中骑手缺乏休息空间、停车区域、充电设施与日常支持系统的问题。设计将被忽视的城市灰空间转化为模块化的补给站网络，为骑手提供休息、充电、停车、交流与社区互动的场所。通过实体空间与智慧数字平台的结合，项目提出一种更具包容性和骑手友好性的城市公共支持系统。
