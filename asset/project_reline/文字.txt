# Project Introduction / 项目介绍

## English

RE:LINE transforms a disused railway corridor in southern Milan into an ecological and cultural spine. The project introduces a continuous slow-mobility network that reconnects fragmented neighbourhoods, farmland and open landscapes while preserving the spatial memory of the former railway. A revitalised quarry lake becomes a major recreational node, integrating water-accessible spaces, community gardens and wetland habitats. Along the wider corridor, repeatable landscape modules adapt to changing urban and rural conditions, turning obsolete infrastructure into a connected public landscape.

## 中文

RE:LINE 以米兰南部一条废弃铁路为线性骨架，将其转化为连接城市社区、农业景观与开放空间的生态文化廊道。项目通过连续的步行和骑行网络重新缝合被基础设施割裂的城乡边缘，同时保留旧铁路的空间记忆。原有采石湖被改造为核心休闲节点，结合亲水空间、社区花园、湿地生境与公共活动场地。沿线设计采用可复制、可调整的景观模块，以适应不同的城市和乡村环境，使废弃基础设施重新成为具有公共价值的绿色网络。

**EN**

The cover presents the abandoned railway as a new linear public landscape. Existing tracks, cycling routes, industrial traces and quarry lakes are layered together to express the transformation of obsolete infrastructure into a slow-mobility and ecological corridor.

**CN**

封面将废弃铁路呈现为新的线性公共景观。旧铁轨、骑行路径、工业遗存与采石湖相互叠加，共同表达废弃基础设施向慢行生态廊道的转化过程。

---

## Image 01 — Overall Strategy / 整体策略

**EN**

The regional strategy identifies the abandoned railway as a continuous landscape spine connecting Porto di Mare, Chiaravalle, farmland areas and Sorigherio. Slow-mobility routes and ecological interventions are coordinated through a sequence of entrances, transitional spaces and public nodes.

**CN**

区域策略将废弃铁路转化为贯穿 Porto di Mare、Chiaravalle、农业区与 Sorigherio 的连续景观骨架。慢行路径与生态修复措施通过入口、过渡空间和公共节点形成完整的线性系统。

---

## Image 02 — Corridor Master Plan and Nodes / 廊道总平面与节点设计

**EN**

The corridor master plan translates the regional concept into a detailed linear landscape system. Selected nodes respond to different spatial conditions, including preserved railway traces, cycling paths, pedestrian routes, seating areas, bridges and connections to surrounding communities.

**CN**

廊道总平面将区域构想落实为具体的线性景观系统。不同节点根据场地条件配置旧铁路遗迹、骑行道、步行路径、休憩空间、跨越设施以及通往周边社区的连接界面。

---

## Image 03 — Quarry Lake Park Master Plan / 采石湖公园总体设计

**EN**

The former quarry lake is reimagined as a recreational and ecological destination. The design integrates a swimming pool, water-accessible platforms, community gardens, activity lawns, wetland planting and a continuous circulation network.

**CN**

原有采石湖被重新塑造为兼具休闲和生态价值的公共目的地。设计整合泳池、亲水平台、社区花园、活动草坪、湿地种植与连续的慢行系统。

---

## Image 04 — Waterfront Park Details / 滨水公园节点深化

**EN**

Detailed plans and sections illustrate the relationship between the swimming pool, service facilities, wetland edges and elevated waterfront platforms. The design creates a gradual transition from active public spaces to restored aquatic habitats.

**CN**

节点深化图展示了泳池、服务设施、湿地边缘与架高滨水平台之间的空间关系。设计在公共活动区域与修复后的水生生境之间建立渐进式过渡。

---

## Image 05 — Repeatable Railway Modules / 铁路廊道模块化设计

**EN**

Four repeatable design modules demonstrate how the former railway can adapt to changing contexts along the corridor. Existing tracks, cycling lanes, pedestrian paths, vegetation buffers and small resting platforms form a flexible landscape toolkit.

**CN**

四组可复制的设计模块展示了废弃铁路如何适应沿线不同环境。原有铁轨、骑行道、步行路径、植被缓冲带与小型休憩平台共同构成灵活的线性景观工具箱。
