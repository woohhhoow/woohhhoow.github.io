Core Philosophy

We shape our environments; therefore, our environments shape us.

I believe that landscape is not merely an aesthetic endeavor, but a catalyst for systemic change. By weaving together site specifics, biological systems, and architectural parameters, the practice aims to create spaces that are both poetic and performative.

The Studio

Wu Haiwen is a landscape architect dedicated to exploring the complex interplay between natural systems, urban infrastructure, and human experience.

Based on rigorous site analysis and a profound respect for materiality, the practice focuses on delivering landscapes that are both performative and poetic.
