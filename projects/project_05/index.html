Built (Phase I)

Reimagining the fragmented urban edge into a resilient wetland system. The design fosters ecological diversity while providing an immersive spatial narrative, turning neglected infrastructure into a vital urban lung.

Parametric Water Logic

The topography is computationally generated to simulate historical flood patterns. By utilizing Grasshopper, the terrain steps are optimized to accommodate a 50-year storm event, creating micro-habitats for native flora and fauna while maintaining human accessibility.
