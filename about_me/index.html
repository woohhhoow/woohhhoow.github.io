Landscape is not a static backdrop, but a dynamic, living system. We design frameworks for nature and humanity to co-author the future.

I am Wu Haiwen, a landscape architect currently pursuing my Master's degree at Politecnico di Milano. My academic and professional journey is driven by a deep fascination with the intersection of natural ecologies, urban infrastructure, and human experience.

Complementing my academic research, I am actively advancing my parametric and systemic design thinking through ASA advanced courses. My practice focuses on delivering landscapes that are both performative and poetic, shifting seamlessly from macro-scale territorial strategies to micro-scale tactile details.
